About Gummy

“Gummy” describes something flexible yet structured, a balance between control and softness. In design, it can represent the tension between order and play
— where structure holds form, but movement keeps it alive.

The letters here are treated as material. Their rhythm, spacing, and scale create a sense of elasticity and sound. This page explores how typography can feel tactile, not just visual.
